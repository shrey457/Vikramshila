# Vikramshila
Vikramshila - The ultimate solution for the disciples of the great Mohit Tyagi YouTube Channel, providing a list of all PCM lectures of the YouTube channel arranged in a sequential manner 
for distraction-free studying. Notes and other resources have been provided for you as well ;)
## Features
- ### Ordered Playlist 
  An ordered playlist of all videos have been provided for Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry subdivided into topics and lectures for better and oriented learning  
- ### Proper Motivation
  A significant hurdle faced by self-studying students is the lack of motivation and objective while studying. Vikramshila ensures that you don't get lost 🫵. 
  A unique problem in Competishun lectures is the number of lectures per playlist which overwhelms most students. But Vikramshila provides the real time metrics per playlist!
- ### Distraction-free Learning 
  Have you ever opened a YouTube lecture only to later find yourself scrolling through shorts an hour later? Not anymore. Vikramshila's in-built player prevents such 
  tragedies. The website design is simple and minimalistic, so that you can focus purely on your dream of IIT Bombay 😎
